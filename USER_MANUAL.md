# User Guide

Quick guide for using the Clinical Management System.

## Getting Started

1. Open your browser and go to `http://127.0.0.1:5000`
2. Login with:
   - Username: `admin` Password: `admin123`
   - OR Username: `nurse1` Password: `nurse123`

## Dashboard

The main page shows:

- Total number of patients
- Today's appointments
- Recent vital signs recorded
- List of upcoming appointments

## Managing Patients

### Add New Patient

1. Click "Patients" in the top menu
2. Click "Add Patient" button
3. Fill in the form (name, age, gender are required)
4. Click "Add Patient" to save

### Edit Patient

- Click the pencil icon next to patient name
- Update fields and click "Update Patient"

### Delete Patient

- Click the trash icon
- Confirm deletion (this also deletes their vitals and appointments)

## Recording Vitals

1. Go to "Vitals" tab
2. Click "Record Vitals"
3. Select patient
4. Enter measurements:
   - Blood Pressure: use format 120/80
   - Heart Rate: beats per minute
   - Temperature: in Celsius
   - Respiratory Rate: breaths per minute
   - Oxygen Saturation: optional, percentage
5. Add notes if needed
6. Click "Record Vitals" to save

**Normal Ranges:**

- BP: 90-120/60-80
- HR: 60-100 bpm
- Temp: 36.1-37.2Â°C
- RR: 12-20/min
- SpO2: 95-100%

## Appointments

### Schedule New

1. Click "Appointments" tab
2. Click "New Appointment"
3. Select patient, date, time
4. Enter reason for visit
5. Click "Schedule Appointment"

### Update Status

- Click green checkmark to mark completed
- Click red X to cancel
- Click trash icon to delete

### Filter Appointments

Use buttons at top to filter by:

- All
- Scheduled (blue)
- Completed (green)
- Cancelled (red)

## Tips

- Always double-check vital signs before saving
- Add notes for unusual readings
- Keep patient info up to date
- Log out when done (click your username, then Logout)

## Problems?

- Can't log in? Check username/password, make sure Caps Lock is off
- Page won't load? Try refreshing (F5)
- Patient not showing? Check spelling or scroll through list
